Creative Whispers, 2016, Collaborative project, Digital collage.

Creative Whispers is a global creative collaborative project connecting writers and artists together. Beginning in New York in 2015, this ongoing project encourages collaboration between two artistic disciplines, and creation of new work. Bouncing from writer, to artist, the project work as a domino effect of inspiration, as a participant you receive a new work, that you must in turn create a work in response to.

This digital collage was in response to a short essay from an anonymous writer.

Untitled, 2019, Seville, Spain, Cyanotype prints using collected local plants.

This is a site specific project that I made while I was living in Seville, Spain, taking part in an internship and training programme with contemporary art gallery Alarcón Criado. These cyanotype prints are made using paper coated with cyanotype solution, local collected plants and the strong Sevillian sun.

A collection of them were exhibited as part of Otra Vida, a group exhibition in Galería Magnetic.

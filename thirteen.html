Research Residency, 2020, Science Gallery Dublin & Trinity College Dublin, Digital photographs documenting research. Materials.

This new area of artistic research explores nature based, foraged, hand-made art materials. My area of investigation, using traditional techniques and recipes as inspiration, uses foraged plants and minerals to create usable mediums. I am interested in exploring nature through materials and processes in my work. Shown here are a selection of photographs documenting the plant based processes and finished mediums that were created throughout the course of this residency, as well as an educational video (shown above or here).

This research was made possible through the Science Gallery Dublin’s Rapid Residency award and funded by Trinity College Dublin Provost’s Fund.

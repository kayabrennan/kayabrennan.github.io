Beyond Dialogue, 2017, Crawford College of Arts & Design, Degree show, Photo plate lithography, embossed prints.

Details of printmaking works made for Beyond Dialogue. This collection of work was inspired by the skin and the sensation of touch. Guided by the philosophy of the senses as written by french philosopher Michel Serres, these hand pulled prints were intended to create an intimate imitation of the skin and question our own spacial and sensual awareness of our bodies.
